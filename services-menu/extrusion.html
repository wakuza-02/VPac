Blown Film Extrusion

Our extrusion department features both monolayer and advanced three-layer co-extrusion lines, giving us the versatility to produce a wide spectrum of polyethylene filmsâ€”from high-volume general-purpose bags to specialized, high-performance packaging.

  • Monolayer Extrusion
  • Three Layer Co-Extrusion

Our films are engineered for purpose. The monolayer extruder provides rapid, reliable production for general-purpose applications like shrink wraps and refuse bags. For more demanding needs, our fully-automated three-layer co-extruder creates films with specific properties like enhanced clarity, strength, or rigidity.

Our key technical capabilities include:

  • Wide operational range: Thickness from 10 to 1,000 microns.
  • Versatile widths up to 3.3 metres to suit any application.
  • Advanced automation for exceptional 3% tolerance control.
  • Specialized material blending for custom film properties (LDPE, LLDPE, HDPE, Metallocenes, and more).

This combination of experience and technology enables us to produce the finest quality flexible product at a very competitive price, from standard fruit & vegetable bags to highly technical lamination films.
