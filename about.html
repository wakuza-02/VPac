About Us

Venk-Pac is one of South Africa’s leading producers of polyethylene films and packaging. Operating from our state-of-the-art plant, we are proud to deliver our products with an unwavering commitment to quality and service that reaches every corner of the nation.

Our clients trust us to deliver consistently performing products all over the world.

We provide the best in innovation, quality and service and offer a wide portfolio of products to help your products and brand stand out from the crowd. We have a programme of continuous investment in our people, production, processes and infrastructure which allows us to operate from one of the most modern film processing plants in the world.

The need for improved product performance, faster packing speeds and the development of leading-edge and cost-effective packaging solutions. Venk-Pac recognises this and is fully equipped to supply packaging solutions that are easier to recycle and more forgiving to process – products that provide both cost and environmental benefits to our clients.

As a family business, we have adopted a caring culture, which utilizes the distinctive competences of all our available resources. Our abilities, along with those of our business partners and suppliers are innovatively combined to offer technically superior products that deliver real benefits to your business.
