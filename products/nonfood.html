Our Products

Non Food

From industrial applications to consumer goods, our non-food packaging is designed for superior protection, durability, and performance. Our solutions include:

E-commerce Mailers

Our light weight, fully recyclable mailers offer the perfect solution to those e-commerce companies looking for sustainable packaging with a low carbon footprint. Our mailers can be printed up to 10 colours and be customised to your exact needs.

Stretch Hood Films

These films need to perform consistently in unforgiving conditions. Our multilayer stretch hood films offer a robust and reliable solution. Tight thickness control and controlled stretch mean you can rely on its performance every time.

Vented FFS Films

Dry Vent is a breathable, tubular FFS film used to package moisture sensitive and powdery products. Breathable slits and gussets inside the bag allows air out and no moisture in. These films can be supplied with recycled content.

Lawn & Garden Films

Our multilayer co-ex films offer the ultimate protection for your products. Tough, durable and consistently performing films, coupled with scratch and rub resistant inks, mean that you can be assured that your packaging will look and perform perfectly throughout your season.

Building & Construction Films

Whether you are looking for an FFS film for packing aggregates or a shrink hood film to securely palletise your goods, we have the right solution for your needs. Films can be supplied printed up to 10 colours and tailored to your requirements.

Tissue & Towel Films

Primary and secondary overwrap films supplied printed up to 10 colours. Our proven formulations deliver exceptional performance on high-speed wrapping lines. These films can be supplied with recycled content and are fully recyclable.
