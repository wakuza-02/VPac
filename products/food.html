Our Products

Food

Our food packaging solutions are engineered to ensure product freshness, safety, and shelf appeal. We offer a wide range of films for various food applications.

Frozen

We have set the benchmark with our high-performance deep freeze films. Extremely forgiving and consistent performance meaning you can rely on our films to pack at optimum speed with no defects. Films can be supplied in high gloss or matt/gloss finish.

Bakery

Our ultra-modern bag makers have the capacity to produce millions of bags per annum. Each bag maker is fitted with supplementary modules to allow us to customise your bag with special features. Our bread film can be printed up to 10 colours and can be fully recycled.

Fresh Produce

To allow for high-speed packing, low SIT and thermal resistance are critical to quality attributes that make this film highly efficient. This film can be supplied with perforations, printed up to 10 colours and can be fully recycled.

Liquid Packaging

Excellent resistance to stress cracking, low SIT and heat resistance for hot-fill applications are some of the key attributes of our films for liquid packaging. We have designed this multilayer film with a circular economy in mind and as such it can be fully recycled.

Shrink Films

Protecting your products is our primary goal. Our ultra-thin films can be supplied with recycled content, be printed up to 10 colours and can be fully recycled, ensuring your products will arrive at their destination as good as the day they left your plant.

Pouch Bags

Make your product the center of attention with films designed for stunning high-definition graphics and a premium feel. While providing excellent protection, our films support convenient features like resealable zippers and tear notches, creating packaging that consumers love to see and use.
