1. Introduction

Welcome to venkpac ("we," "us," "our"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have in relation to it. This policy applies to all information collected through our website (www.venkpac.com) and any related services, sales, marketing, or events.

By using our website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. What Information Do We Collect?

a) Personal Information You Disclose to Us

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you create an account, make a purchase, subscribe to our newsletter, contact us through our contact form, or participate in a survey.

This information may include:

  • Contact Data: Name, email address, mailing address, phone number.
  • Account Data: Username, password.
  • Payment Data: Payment card details (processed securely by our third-party payment processors). We do not store your full credit card number on our servers.

b) Information Automatically Collected

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information about your device and browsing activity. This information may include:

  • Log and Usage Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, pages viewed, time spent on pages, and the dates/times of your visits.
  • Device Data: Information about the computer or mobile device you use to access our website.
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: We use cookies to track activity on our service. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies, but some parts of our site may not function correctly.

3. How Do We Use Your Information?

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our service.
  • Manage your account and process transactions.
  • Communicate with you regarding updates, products, or support.
  • Send you marketing materials, which you can opt-out of at any time.
  • Improve our website by analyzing usage trends.

4. How Do We Share Your Information?

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with trusted third parties who perform services for us, such as:

  • Payment Processors: To securely process your payments.
  • Shipping Carriers: To deliver your orders.
  • Analytics Providers: To help us understand our website usage (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Legal Obligations: We may disclose your information where we are legally required to do so.

5. Data Security

We have implemented appropriate security measures to protect the security of any personal information we process. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.
