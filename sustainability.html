SUSTAINABILITY

Venk-Pac Stands For Sustainability

Ours is an industry with an inevitable impact on the environment, but we take our responsibility to protect it seriously, reducing any effect to an absolute minimum.

At the heart of our program is our investment in a cutting-edge Erema degrassing recycler. This isn't just about processing our own scrap; it's about leading by example. This advanced system allows us to convert all internal production materials back into high-quality, reusable pellets.

Our aims are clear, and we achieve them by adhering to the highest standards:

  • We consider environmental impacts from the very beginning of any new product development.
  • We constantly work to improve the energy efficiency of our processing equipment.
  • We reduce material consumption by re-using and recycling wherever practical.
  • We encourage our employees and partners to act in an environmentally responsible way.

This commitment extends to our partners. We offer a buy-back program for surplus or redundant plastic stock, helping our clients meet their own sustainability goals while participating in a true circular economy.

Our commitment to sustainability is built into every stage of our manufacturing process. We believe that true environmental responsibility is achieved through intelligent technology, a closed-loop process, and a partnership approach that delivers tangible benefits.

Technology

We invest in state-of-the-art recycling systems to ensure maximum efficiency, material purity, and the highest quality in our finished products.

Process

Our goal is zero waste. We operate a closed-loop system where all internal production scrap is reprocessed and converted back into valuable raw material.

Impact

This responsible approach reduces our carbon footprint, enhances cost-effectiveness, and allows us to offer innovative buy-back programs for our clients' surplus materials.

About Us

As one of South Africa’s leading producers of innovative polyethylene films, we deliver exceptional quality and service to clients across the nation and around the globe.
