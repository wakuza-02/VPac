About us

Venk – Pac provides the complete plastic packaging Solutions from its Factory in South Africa. A combination of cutting edge technology, stringent quality standards and over 40 years of experience in the plastic industry, makes Venk – Pac the supplier of choice to Africa and beyond.

We provide a cost effective range of products which optimise the packing and distribution processes and offer effective protection as well as strong on shelf appearance across a diverse range of market segments. We are strategically located to service our the packaging needs of our Customers.

Venk – Pac is an ISO accredited company, 100% black owned and is BBBEE compliant.

Our combination of experience and technology enables us to produce the finest quality flexible product at a very competitive price. We believe we can supply your needs and provide significant cost savings to your business. We specialise in keeping our Customers updated on the developments in the flexible packaging industry, thus accessing them to innovative products whilst saving them money.

Our Commitment to a Circular Economy

Recycling Technology: The Erema System
At the heart of our Green Initiatives program is our investment in a cutting-edge Erema degrassing recycler. This advanced technology is renowned for its ability to produce exceptionally clean and consistent recycled materials, allowing us to maintain the highest quality standards in our finished products.

Processing Capabilities: No Waste Left Behind
Our in-house system is engineered to handle the full spectrum of our production materials. It efficiently converts all scrap, including complex printed films, back into a valuable raw material. This closed-loop process ensures that resources are maximized and nothing is sent to landfill.

Shared Benefits: A Sustainable Partnership
This process is not only environmentally responsible but also economically sound. It makes our production more efficient and cost-effective, a benefit we pass on to our partners. Furthermore, we invite our clients to participate in our circular model through a buy-back program, where we purchase their surplus or redundant plastic stock, helping them meet their own sustainability goals while creating a new revenue opportunity.
